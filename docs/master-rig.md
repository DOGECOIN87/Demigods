All collection traits must align to one immutable 1254 × 1254 front-facing master rig. The approved proportionally rebased integer anchors below are canonical.

| Anchor | Coordinate |
|---|---:|
| Canvas center | X 627 |

| Viewer-right hand | X 850, Y 772 |
| Foot baseline | Y 1139 |
| Maximum bounds | X 233–1021, Y 129–1139 |

## Rules

- Front-facing orthographic appearance only.
- No yaw, pitch, roll, tilt, pose drift, scale drift, or perspective variation.
- Trait layers must retain their full-canvas coordinates rather than being tightly cropped.
- Use hidden overlaps beneath adjoining layers to prevent seams.
- A guide body may be used during construction but must be removed from isolated exports.
- Approved hand poses are separate templates and may not alter head, torso, legs, scale, or baseline.
